LocalDocuments

On-device document scanner powered by ML Kit. Scan physical documents using your camera — no internet required, 100% private.

ML Kit On-Device Android 8.0+ Material 3
Download APK View on GitHub

Screenshots

Features

No Camera Permission Needed

Google Play Services handles the camera. Your app never needs direct camera access.

Automatic Capture

Document detection enables automatic capture. Point and scan without tapping a button.

Edge Detection & Crop

Accurate edge detection automatically crops your document at the optimal boundary.

Filters & Cleaning

Apply grayscale, auto-enhance, remove shadows, erase stains and fingers with ML-powered cleaning.

JPEG & PDF Output

Export scans as JPEG images or a multi-page PDF document. Share directly from the app.

Gallery Import

Import existing photos from your gallery to convert them into scanned documents.

Page Limit

Set a maximum number of pages per scan session. Supports multi-page document scanning.

100% On-Device

All scanning, filtering, and OCR processing happens on your device. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.
